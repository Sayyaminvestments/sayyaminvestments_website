About Our Company

Sayyam Investments Private Limited is a Non-Deposit Taking Non-Banking Finance Company (NBFC) duly registered with the Reserve Bank of India (RBI), with the head office in Guwahati, Assam.

We are engaged in the business of providing loans. Our services are aimed at fulfilling the aspirations and dreams of millions of our customers. We have developed a mobile app called “PayRupik” to cater the personal financial needs of the Indian public.

Payrupik enhances the experience of obtaining instant personal loans up to INR 20,000 within minutes on your mobile phone, here you can avail of loans with low interest, early repayment, and a fast process. For more assistance our customer service is very professional and will actively solve all your problems.

The loan application process is completely transparent and straightforward, facilitating the speedy disbursal of loans. Further, it’s not required to submit any collateral to get a loan from our PayRupik App.


Mission

Facilitation of the financial welfare of our customers by advancing instant credit at the utmost convenience.
