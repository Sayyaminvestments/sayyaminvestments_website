Sayyam Investments Private Limited began operating as a non-deposit-taking non-banking financing firm on October 8, 2012, headquartered in Guwahati, Assam and received RBI certification in 2014. In 2021, We developed a product to assist the general public with their financial needs. Hence, “PayRupik” a mobile app-based service, to meet the personal financial demands of the Indian population. In order to achieve this, we launched this Instant Personal Loan platform for self-employed and salaried professionals. On this platform, you may submit an application for a Personal Loan of up to INR 20,000. We provide our customers a flexible and customised lending facilities with hassle-free online documentation. Furthermore, there is no requirement for collateral when applying for a loan. We currently have 1 million+ downloads, a Google Play store rating of 4.4 stars.


As it is often said, "If you want to lift yourself up, lift someone else." We, At Sayyam Investments, uphold this belief and have a dedicated team for each department which ensures seamless & harmonious functioning of the business. We have a team of experts who work extremely hard to excel at what we do. Sayyam strives to offer its customers the finest credit services in a timely and effective manner. To that end, we have one of the best teams to handle everything. By giving flexible working hours, significant paid time off, meeting individual wants and requirements, and fostering a supportive work environment where personal and family demands are understood, we have a fantastic workplace that is flexible to fit employees' job needs and promotes a work-life balance.
